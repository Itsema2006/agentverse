Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all interactions with AgentVerse, including website usage, AI agent interactions, support requests, integrations, and user dashboards.

We collect information you provide directly and data collected automatically to provide, improve, and secure our services.

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect your data and continuously monitor for threats and vulnerabilities.
